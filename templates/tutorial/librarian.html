Librarian Operations Guide

Efficiently manage circulation and user accounts.

Circulation Desk

  • Issuing Books: Go to Management > Issue Book. Search for a member by username and scan the Book ISBN. The system will auto-check borrow limits.
  • Returns: Go to Management > Return Book. Enter the username to find active loans. Click "Return". If the book is overdue, a fine alert will appear offering "Pay Now" or "Pay Later" options.

User Management

Navigate to Manage Users to view all members. Here you can:

  • View current subscription tiers and borrowing history.
  • Update a member's subscription tier using the dropdown.
  • Block/Unblock members who violate policies.
  • Clear outstanding fines manually.

Catalog Management

Use Add Book to register new inventory. You can use the camera feature to scan ISBN barcodes directly. Editing and deleting books can be done from the Book Detail page.
