Member User Guide

Welcome to Archivus! This guide will help you navigate your borrowing experience.

1

Finding Books

Navigate to the Browse Catalog page to view our collection. You can search by Title, Author, or Category. Click on any book card to see detailed information, including availability and location.

2

Borrowing & Reservations

To borrow a book, simply visit the library counter with the book. Our librarians will issue it to your account.

If a book is marked Out of Stock, you can click the yellow Reserve Book button on its detail page. You will be notified when it becomes available.

3

My Books & Renewals

Go to My Books to see your current loans and due dates. If you need more time, click the Renew button next to an item. Note that renewals are only possible if the book hasn't been reserved by another member.
