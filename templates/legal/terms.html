By registering as a member of the Archivus Library System, you agree to abide by these Terms of Service and all applicable library policies. Failure to comply may result in the suspension or termination of your borrowing privileges.

2. Membership Responsibilities

  • Account Security: You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials. Do not share your login details.
  • Contact Information: You must keep your email and phone number current to receive overdue notices. Failure to receive a notice does not exempt you from fines.
  • Card Usage: Membership privileges are non-transferable. You may not borrow books on behalf of others using your account.

3. Borrowing & Returns

Members may borrow items up to the limit defined by their Membership Tier. All items must be returned by the due date specified at checkout.

  • Condition: Items must be returned in the same condition as borrowed. Users will be charged the full replacement cost for lost or damaged items.
  • Fines: Late returns will incur a daily fine as set by the Library Configuration. Borrowing privileges are suspended if outstanding fines exceed the allowable threshold.
  • Renewals: Items may be renewed if they have not been reserved by another member.

The Library Administration reserves the right to terminate any membership at its discretion for violation of these terms. Upon termination, all borrowed items must be returned immediately.
