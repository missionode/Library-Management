Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Archivus Library Management System. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our members' personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our library services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Identification: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address provided during registration.
  • Library Activity: Records of books borrowed, returned, reserved, and any associated fines or fees.
  • Account Credentials: Encrypted passwords and authentication tokens used to secure your account.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information solely for the purpose of managing library operations, including:

  • Facilitating book loans and returns.
  • Sending notifications regarding due dates, reservations, and overdue items.
  • Analyzing library usage trends to improve our collection (data is anonymized for reports).
  • Managing membership tiers and access privileges.

4. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your reading history is treated as confidential and is not shared with third parties unless required by law.

5. Your Rights

As a library member, you have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information via your Profile page.
  • Update your contact details to ensure you receive important notifications.
  • Request an export of your borrowing history.
  • Request account deletion (subject to returning all borrowed items and clearing outstanding fines).

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the Library Administration at privacy@archivus-systems.com.
